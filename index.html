About

I work on online decision-making under uncertainty, with a focus on applications to classic problems in operations research. I am especially interested in online matching problems, with current work motivated by housing applications. I am also working on using AI to solve open problems in operations research. Alongside research, I have a strong interest in teaching and mentoring students.
